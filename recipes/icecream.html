Banana Ice Cream

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and chop.
  2. Put the bananas in an airtight container.
  3. Freeze the banana pieces for at least 2 hours.
  4. Pulse the frozen banana pieces in a small food processor or powerful blender.
  5. Keep blending until creamy texture.
  6. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until solid.
